MSc in Data Science in India

What is MSc in Data Science?

The MSc in Data Science is a postgraduate program that focuses on the study of data processing, machine learning, data mining, and analytics, with applications in fields such as business, healthcare, and artificial intelligence.

Top MSc in Data Science Colleges in India

  • Indian Statistical Institute (ISI), Kolkata
  • Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kharagpur
  • University of Delhi
  • University of Hyderabad
  • Jawaharlal Nehru University (JNU), Delhi

Entrance Exams for MSc in Data Science in India

The entrance exams for MSc in Data Science depend on the university. Some common exams are:

  • ISI Admission Test
  • JNU Entrance Exam
  • DUET (Delhi University Entrance Test)

How to Study for MSc in Data Science

Prepare by mastering the following areas:

  • Statistics and Probability
  • Programming Languages (Python, R, SQL)
  • Machine Learning Algorithms
  • Data Visualization and Analysis
