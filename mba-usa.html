MBA in the USA

What is an MBA?

The Master of Business Administration (MBA) is one of the most sought-after degrees in the world. It focuses on business leadership, entrepreneurship, and management skills, making graduates ideal for top corporate roles.

Top MBA Colleges in the USA

  • Harvard Business School
  • Stanford Graduate School of Business
  • University of Pennsylvania (Wharton)
  • MIT Sloan School of Management
  • Columbia Business School

Entrance Exams for MBA in the USA

The primary entrance exam for MBA in the USA is the GMAT (Graduate Management Admission Test). Some schools also accept GRE scores.

How to Study for MBA in the USA

For the GMAT exam, focus on:

  • Quantitative Reasoning
  • Verbal Reasoning
  • Integrated Reasoning
  • Analytical Writing Assessment
