MTech in India

What is MTech?

The Master of Technology (MTech) is a postgraduate degree program that focuses on technical knowledge and specialization in fields like Computer Science, Electronics, Civil Engineering, and Mechanical Engineering.

Top MTech Colleges in India

  • Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Delhi
  • Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Bombay
  • Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kanpur
  • Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Kharagpur
  • Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Madras

Entrance Exams for MTech in India

The main entrance exam for MTech courses is the GATE (Graduate Aptitude Test in Engineering). The exam tests the knowledge of candidates in various technical subjects.

How to Study for MTech Entrance Exams

To prepare for GATE, focus on the following areas:

  • Engineering Mathematics
  • General Aptitude
  • Subject-specific Knowledge (based on your engineering discipline)
